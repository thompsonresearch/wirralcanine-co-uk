Wirral Canine Counselling

Founded in 2023 by Emma, Wirral Canine Counselling (Services) aims to provide comprehensive solutions for all aspects of the dog industry. Our services are designed to meet the diverse needs of dog owners, ensuring the highest quality of care and training for their beloved pets.

Our mission is to enhance the well-being and happiness of dogs through professional, reliable, and compassionate services. We strive to build a community where dogs and their owners thrive together.

Why choose us?

We are a highly qualified and trained team. Emma holds both an FdSc ​and BSc in animal behaviour and clinical canine behaviour. Emma’s ​partner, Matt is a current doctoral student and visiting lecturer. Matt ​holds a BSc and MSc in Wildlife Conservation and Ecology.

We are client-centered and dogs in our care are treated as our own. This is shown through our 4 star rating on our canine boarding licence from Wirral Borough Council.
