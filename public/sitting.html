Pet Sitting

Sometimes your pets may love the creature comforts of their home.

At Wirral Canine Counselling, we offer pet sitting for your pets in their home. Our pet sitting service includes walking, feeding, play, enrichment, toilet breaks, and cuddles.

We will 'pop-in' to your home periodically to check on your pet and spend time keeping them company during the day.

Some people prefer their pets to stay at home overnight, and we ensure your pet has everything needed for the night. Alternatively, you can arrange overnight home boarding with us at our home.
