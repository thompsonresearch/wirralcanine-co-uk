Canine Behaviour

Emma is a qualified canine behaviourist having achieved a BSc in Clinical ​Canine Behaviour at the University of Chester. Also, Emma holds membership ​with the Pet Professional Network (PPN).

At Wirral Canine Counselling we offer canine behaviour sessions ranging ​from minor training issues to complex behavioural support. We take a client-centred approach to put you at the heart of your dog's progression.

Our aim is to work together to enhance the situation through better ​understanding, ultimately strengthening the bond with your furry friend.
