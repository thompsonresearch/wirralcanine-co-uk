Dog Boarding

Home dog boarding gives your dog a space to relax and unwind without the ​noise of kennels and the love of our own. Your dog will be given their own ​space but the opportunity to socialise with our older resident dogs.

We will care for your dog in our home and they will have access to a secure ​rear garden and living space. Home dog boarding includes daily local walks, ​grooming, feeding, health checks and play. These are tailored to your dogs ​individual needs, just let us know.

Additionally, we have a range of optional extras, including sports and adventure ​walks, use of enclosed fields, training and behaviour services.

🐾

Facilities

The ground floor of our home is open to your dog with a secure rear garden. The rear door will remain open on warmer days to give your dog freedom to road in and outside the house, especially when needing the toilet. Our front living space is our designated 'dog space' and your dog will have their own faux leather sofa and choices of multiple dog beds. This room is connected directly to our living space so your dog will always have our company during their stay.

The rear garden is a mixed-use area and has areas of sun and shade. Your dog will have access to a small dog paddling pool for warmer days. Our lawn is ideal for your dog to rest and play. Angled mesh is used around the top of our fencing. This is primarily for our resident cat but also aids to prevent dogs escaping (some can climb!)

Our home has a front porch and rear 'lean to' extension. This means we have double door systems front and back. This keeps your dog extra safe for escaping. We must stress when visiting and leaving our property please close the inner doors, before proceeding to open the external doors too.

Multiple waterbowls are situated around the home so your dog will never be without water. We clean and steralise these bowls daily.

Your trial stay

We require a trial stay prior to boarding and the ​timescale and date for the trial is agreed upon. Please ​note there is a fee for the trial stay based on trial stay ​type and time.

Please talk to us about arranging your dog’s trial. Successful trials will see your dog boarding with us.

🐾

Optional Extras

Optional extras incur additional fees on top of your ​boarding fee. This will be to cover fuel, travel, location ​specific purchase (e.g. secure field hire) and training or ​behaviour services.

Please ask us about optional extras when booking your ​dogs boarding stay with us.
