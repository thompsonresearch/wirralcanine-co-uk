Dog Walking

Exercise and enrichment are essential for the health and wellbeing of dogs. Just like us, dogs enjoy the outdoors to sniff, run, roll, and play.

We provide a dog walking service for daily dogs and canine behaviour walks (for those with challenging behaviour on walks).

We also offer dog hikes and adventures provided by Matt. Hikes and adventures take your dog on longer 'adventure-like' walks in places such as Delamere forest, woodland, rugged, and coastal terrain walks. These walks are subject to availability and will be provided on a first come first served basis via our Facebook page. We will only be able to take dogs in peak health and with no underlying or serious health conditions. Please speak to us about upcoming dog hikes.
