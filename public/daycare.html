Daycare (Day Boarding)

Home doggy daycare gives your dog a space to relax and unwind without the ​noise of kennels and the love of our own. Your dog will be given their own ​space but the opportunity to socialise with our older resident dogs.

Our daycare provides a home away from home for your dog throughout the day, with enriching activities, play and socialisation. Similar to our overnight boarding, your dog will have contiuous access (weather permitting) to our rear garden for the duration of the day, walking, love and attention, the same as our own.

🐾

Daycare Hours

Our daycare hours are:

08:30am to 17:00pm daily (including weekends)

Please be advised that daycare rates transition to overnight boarding rates after 5:00 PM. Late pickups can interfere with the comfort and routine of our overnight guests. Therefore, if you require daycare services beyond 5:00 PM, an overnight boarding fee will be applied to ensure we can maintain the highest standard of care for all of our clients. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
